Machine Clippings

A recurrent neural network1 trained on five years of headlines, article summaries, locations and bylines from the New York Times 2 was used to create this archive of imaginary newspaper clippings. Starting with no understanding of the English language, the network learns to immitate the content and tone of the source material.

  • Karpathy, Andrei (2015): The unreasonable Effectiveness of Recurrent Neural Networks. Using a Tensorflow implementation by Sherjil Ozair.
  • Obtained through the New York Times API
